Who We Are

Sonos is a fictional studio creating headphones and home audio products that feel quiet, intelligent, and enduring. Our work is shaped by the belief that premium sound should never come at the expense of clarity, comfort, or restraint.

What We Value

We focus on balanced acoustics, tactile finishes, and interfaces that disappear into use. Every product is designed to feel effortless in the hand and confident in the room, with details that reward close attention.

How We Work

Our fictional process combines industrial design, acoustic tuning, and careful material studies into a single product language. The result is a portfolio meant to look composed, sound expansive, and age gracefully.
